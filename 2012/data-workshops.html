Data Workshops, 26 July

We’ve scheduled the most upvoted of the hands-on workshops from the session proposals into a separate day,  on Thursday, 26 July, one day before the main event.

When: July 26, 2012, 9 AM to 6 PM
Where: The Energy & Resources Institute, 4th Main, 2nd Cross, and the Centre for Internet and Society, 2nd C Cross, next to Domlur Club, Domlur 2nd Stage, Bangalore – 560 071

The workshops are free for anyone with a ticket to The Fifth Elephant, but venue capacity is limited. You must click on an individual workshop to get to its respective RSVP page.  Please only RSVP to the workshops you are really interested in.
