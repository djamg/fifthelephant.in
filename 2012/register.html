Register for The Fifth Elephant

This event is participant-supported. Your ticket money helps cover the cost of running the event and ensures that you are our highest priority at all times. Your ticket gets you full access to the main event and free registration to the pre-event hacknights and workshops (limited capacity).

Ticket prices will go up as the event dates get closer, so buy today to get the best price.

If your employer is paying for your ticket, we encourage you to get a corporate ticket. These are billed directly to your employer and the extra money is used to cover travel and accommodation for speakers who don’t live in Bangalore. Your support will be acknowledged at the event. Please write to us at info@hasgeek.com for details.

Our privacy policy: We will never share your details without your explicit approval. All offers from partners and sponsors require you to accept them.

We’ll give you an NFC-enabled badge at the event. If you meet a sponsor with an interesting proposition, tap your badge on their ContactPoint reader to share contact information. It’s easy, painless, and leaves you in full control of whom you share your details with.

Cancelling your tickets: If you need to cancel your ticket please email info@hasgeek.com with the Order ID you received and the name of the attendee.
