NIMHANS Convention Centre

Conference & Workshops

The NIMHANS Convention Centre is one of the best conference venues in Bangalore.

With three large capacity auditoriums, spacious exhibition area for sponsors and plenty of open space for conference attendees to interact and hack, the venue can conveniently hold 1,500 people without the space appearing crowded.

The Convention Centre is on Marigowda Road, past the main NIMHANS campus when you approach from Dairy Circle.

Getting There

From Airport

NIMHANS Convention Centre is approximately 41 kms away from the Bangalore International Airport (BIA). You can hire a cab from the taxi lane or board an air-conditioned city bus (route BIAS1) from the BMTC bus stand at the airport to reach the venue directly. It takes about 1-1.5 hours to reach the Convention Centre from the airport.

Alternate bus routes BIAS9, BIAS 8A will bring you to Majestic bus terminal from where you can board a bus to reach the venue.

From Majestic

NIMHANS Convention Centre is about 8.5 kms from Majestic bus terminus. Buses to the venue depart from platform numbers 13 and 14 (verification of platform numbers required). Bus routes 365, 369D, 369F, 369C, 360, 171, 171c, 165, 168 will get you to the venue directly.

On a regular day it takes about 33-35 minutes to reach the Convention Centre from the Majestic bus terminus. If you have trouble getting to the venue, try using Bangalore Transport Information System.

The Bak Bak Bar

Party Venue

Bak Bak Bar is a newly opened bar and restaurant in Koramangala. The quirky decor and comfortable atmosphere makes this place an ideal venue to wind down after a full day of talks. Food and drinks are on the house. Talk to speakers, fellow participants and sponsors in a relaxed, informal environment.

Note that the party is open only for the conference attendees. Attendees must show their lanyards at the entrance.

July 12th, 7pm – 11pm

Reaching from NIMHANS Convention Centre

By Foot

Bak Bak Bar is located about 1 km away from the NIMHANS Convention Center. Walk down the road towards Koramangala. Pass NIMHANS hospital on the right. You will pass Christ College followed by the Chocolate Room. Bak Bak Bar is at the next junction.

By Car/Autorickshaw

Get out of the Convention Center and follow the road to a small junction. Take a U-turn and drive down the road. Take the flyover and continue till you hit the next traffic signal. The signal is easy to miss. Watch out for Bak Bak Bar on your right. Traffic will be heavy between 6 and 7 PM. Parking is available in the venue basement. Alternatively, you can park inside Forum Mall which is diagonally across the road.
