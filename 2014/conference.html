Introduction

The Fifth Elephant is India's largest gathering of big data and analytics practitioners. The theme of this year's edition is analytics. Speakers will share practical experiences with data mining, machine learning and building technology for analytics.

The conference has two tracks: infrastructure for analytics and applications.

Analytics infrastructure: platforms, tools and in-house solutions.

Data mining and machine learning: applications in different domains.
